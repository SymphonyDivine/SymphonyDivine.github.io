The Easiest Cinnamon Rolls

Skip the long process of making dough from scratch and still get the magic of fresh cinnamon rolls. Using King's Hawaiian rolls, you'll have soft, fluffy, and gooey cinnamon rolls in no time at all. Every bite is filled with buttery cinnamon sugar and finished with sweet cream cheese frosting that melts right in your mouth.

PREP TIME: 20 MINUTES             COOK TIME: 10 MINUTES
TOTAL TIME: 30 MINUTES           YIELDS: 12 ROOLS

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 (12-pack) King's Hawaiian Original Sweet Rolls

CINNAMON BUTTER

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

CREAM CHEESE FROSTING

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tablespoon heavy cream

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.

  2. Keep the rolls together as a slab and transfer to a baking tray lined with parchment paper. With a sharp knife, cut into each roll in a swirl pattern about 3/4 of the way down, starting from the outside moving towards the center, without slicing all the way through.

  3. In a bowl, whisk together the melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon until it resembles lava. Pour the mixture all over the rolls, letting it seep into the cuts. Allow the rolls to soak for 10 to 15 minutes.

  4. Bake for 10 minutes, until golden on top and gooey inside.

  5. While baking, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and heavy cream, mixing until fluffy.

  6. Spread the frosting over the warm rolls or serve on the side for dipping.
